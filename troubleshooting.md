# SoleMate User Guide

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account
1. Click "Register" in the top right corner
2. Enter your name, email, and password
3. Click "Create Account"

### Browsing Products
- Browse by category: Men, Women, Kids
- Use filters: Size, Price, Brand
- Search: Enter keywords in search bar

### Making a Purchase
1. Select product size and quantity
2. Click "Add to Cart"
3. Review cart and click "Checkout"
4. Enter shipping address
5. Select payment method
6. Confirm order

### Tracking Orders
1. Login to your account
2. Click "My Orders"
3. View order status and tracking number

### Managing Account
- Update profile: Account > Profile
- Change password: Account > Security
- View wishlist: Account > Wishlist

## Tips for Best Deals
- Check "Sale" category for discounted items
- Sign up for newsletter for exclusive offers
- Add items to wishlist for price drop alerts
